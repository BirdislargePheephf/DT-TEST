Introduction

THE YEAR IS 2027

The United States Emergency Government has successfully unified America claiming itself to be the new successor of the former government, the Emergency Government announced to the world in the U.N Headquarters in early 2027 to start their new "Campaign for Global Peace" due to the disbanding of NATO after the United States collapsed in late 2021.

"We did not choose to be the guardians of the gate, but there is no one else" — Lyndon B. Johnson

NATO's successor "the U.N Strategic Coalition for Peace" is established with the U.S as its leader, with more than 100+ members it started one of the largest global security and counterterrorism efforts since the War on Terror.
